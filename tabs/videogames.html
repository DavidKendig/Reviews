Crimson Skies: High Road to Revenge

2003 · FASA Studio / Microsoft

Xbox
★★★★★

An absolute gem of the original Xbox era. Arcade-style aerial combat in a gorgeous dieselpunk 1930s alternate history. The dogfights are exhilarating and the world is brimming with personality.
