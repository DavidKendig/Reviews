Card Games

Trading and collectible card games I've played, rated, and reviewed.

Flesh and Blood

2019 · Legend Story Studios

★★★★☆

A refreshing take on TCGs with its unique pitch/resource system. Combat feels visceral and strategic. The hero-focused gameplay and beautiful card art set it apart.

Magic: The Gathering

1993 · Wizards of the Coast

★★★★★

The granddaddy of all trading card games and still the best. Infinite strategic depth, incredible art, and a thriving community. Commander is the perfect way to play.
